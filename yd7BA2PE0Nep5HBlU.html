LapseLock: ML-Powered Donor Retention Platform

Welcome to LapseLock

LapseLock is a cutting-edge machine learning platform designed to predict and prevent donor lapse for IsraelGives and its non-profit clients. Our advanced ML algorithms identify at-risk donors before they lapse, enabling proactive retention strategies and personalized re-engagement campaigns.

Key Features

  • Predictive Lapse Analytics
  • Personalized Retention Strategies
  • Automated Engagement Workflows
  • Donor Segmentation and Clustering
  • Retention Performance Dashboard
  • Continuous Learning and Optimization
