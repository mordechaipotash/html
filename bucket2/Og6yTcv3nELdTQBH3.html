LapseLock: ML-Powered Donor Lapse Prediction and Prevention Platform

Revolutionize Your Donor Retention Strategy

LapseLock is a specialized machine learning platform designed to predict and prevent donor lapse for IsraelGives and its non-profit clients. By leveraging advanced ML algorithms, LapseLock identifies at-risk donors before they lapse, enabling proactive retention strategies and personalized re-engagement campaigns.
